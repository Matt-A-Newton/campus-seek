Campus Seek

What is Campus Seek?

Campus Seek is a fun and interactive way to explore campus while getting out and excersising!

The goal of Campus Seek is to help students and faculty improve their physical and mental wellbeing by encouraging them to be more active in their everyday life.

How does Campus Seek do this?

By using a modern take on the classic game "Hide and Seek". Scattered around campus there are fliers with QR codes. Each flier is labeled with a number to denote which flier it is. Can you find them all?

Hint: The first one is in the Building for Academic and Student Services (BASS)! Tell others which fliers you've found on Twitter ("X") and other Social Media using the hastag "#campus_seek"!

Why is exersise important?

College students and faculty are under a lot of stress which can lead to depresion and other mental health issues. Excersising is a great way to destress and improve your mental health.
